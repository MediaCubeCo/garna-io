Everything You Need to Manage Small Business Payroll

We have separated the important processes to make it easier for you to manage both full-time employees and freelancers

1. Hire and pay employees (EoR)

Full-time recruitment without opening legal entities and hiring legal and accounting consultants abroad

  • Employer of Record We become the official employer for your employees in 150+ countries.
  • Taxes and benefits We take care of the calculation of contributions, health insurance and pension contributions according to local laws.
  • 100% Compliance All employment contracts and reports are executed flawlessly and are available to you at any time.

2. Pay contractors and freelancers (CoR)

Scale payments to team members in one click wherever they are

  • Flexible payments Pay via SWIFT, SEPA or in the crypto. Your performers can receive money in a convenient way.
