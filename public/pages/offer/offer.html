Common questions

Everything you need to know about getting paid

How quickly do I receive my money?
Most US domestic payments are processed within 24 hours, and you can withdraw them to your local bank account instantly.
Is there a fee for receiving payments?
We charge a transparent fee with no hidden exchange rate markups, so you see exactly what you get before you withdraw.
Do I need a registered company to use Garna?
No, Garna is built for international individuals and contractors to accept payments from US clients without needing a US entity or SSN.
Is my money safe?
Yes, we use bank-grade security and fully compliant payment rails to ensure your funds and data are protected at every step.
Which payment methods from US clients are supported?
We support ACH and Wire transfers, which are the standard domestic payment methods for US companies.
Can I work with clients who only pay US entities?
Yes, Garna provides you with the necessary details to receive payments from clients who strictly require a US-based recipient.
Do you provide tax documentation for my local authorities?
Yes, Garna generates the required documents to help you easily report your income to your local tax office.
Are there any monthly subscription costs?
No, Garna follows a pay-as-you-go model with no monthly fees—you only pay a small commission when you receive a payment.
Is Garna available for freelancers from my country?
Garna is designed for international contractors and freelancers worldwide who want to scale their business with US-based clients.
What if my client requires a W-9 form?
Garna simplifies the compliance process by handling the necessary documentation required by US clients for domestic payments.
